FREEBIES!

You will never be charged by Evomox and you get to keep every product you test. All products that we deliver to our product testers have passed safety testing. We have a zero-tolerance policy on safety testing and all of our clients must provide us with a Safety Data Sheet to ensure the products you test are fit for human use. The product (almost) always arrive to you de-branded. This ensures your opinion of a particular brand does not effect your opinion and allows you to judge the product on it's own merit.
